The Jeb Quest

Plot: The Quest / Comedy

Setting: Snited Otes Af Umerica; 6120

Main Characters: Beb Jush

Characters:The God Emperor (Drump), Crooked Ice Witch (Killary), The Goblin of the Guac (Rarco Mubio), Killer of the Zodiac (Truz), ★★★★★★★ The Turtle (Beb Jush)

STORY:

Beb Jush, nicknamed The Turtle, leads a group named the Slow and Steady, whose main purpose is to overthrow the current ruler of Umerica, Tonald Drump, or commonly known as the God Emperor. Just recently, the God Emperor announced that he would relinquish his rule to anybody that managed to reach his armed fortress, claiming that “Nobody builds fortresses better than me.” Jush took up the gauntlet, going up against the people who also want to become the ruler of Umerica, Killary Hlinton, also known as the Hider of Mails; Rarco Mubio, the Goblin of the Guac; and Ced Truz, the Killer of the Zodiac. Along with the Slow and Steady, Jush defeats the three and then reaches the fortress of Drump. After a series of choices, Jush eventually wins the right to rule Umerica and Drump had his skin turn into an orange color.
